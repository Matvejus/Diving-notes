## Technical diving fundamentals
1. An elite diver will have a focus on their dive that is:
	Mission oriented approach and technical approach. Diving is means to an end to access something distinct and special underwater. Focus on a dive and 360 control all the time.
2. The elite diver will focus on what fundamentals of diving?
	1. Buoyancy
	2. Swimming
	3. Trim
	4. Breathing
3. How can lung volume be used to control buoyancy?
	1. Inhale go up, exhale go down
4. While using Ideal breathing the dive should fill their lungs from the bottom and empty them from the top
5. 2 reasons to deviate from the ideal breathing:
	1. On purpose, consciously
	2. On rebreather
6. While diving, when should be hand be used for positioning and turning? - Never
7. What are the advantage of diving in working position? - streamlined, less effort, no dangling equipment that can snatch.
8. What body position should a diver be in during the working portion of a dive near an impactable environment? - flat, arched back, knees bent.
9. Is there a time in diving when the foundational skills can be ignored? - no
